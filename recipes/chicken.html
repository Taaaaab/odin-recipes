Sheet-Pan Chicken with Zucchini

Description:

Simple recipe for seasoned chicken thighs and drumsticks roasted alongside zucchini. Can add rice or pasta as well, comfortable serves two to three.

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 pounds bone-in, skin on chicken thighs and drumsticks
  • Salt and freshly ground blank pepper
  • 1 3/4 pounds zucchini, sliced into 1-inch chunks
  • 2 fat garlic cloves, finely grated or minced
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 425 degrees. Pat chicken dry with paper towels and season all over with 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper. Place zucchini on a rimmed sheet pan and season with a little more salt and pepper
  2. In a small bowl, combine garlic, oregano and red pepper flakes. Whisk in oil. Add chicken to the pan with the zucchini pieces and pour garlic mixture over all, tossing until well coated. Spread chicken and zucchini in a single layer and roast until chicken is cooked through and zucchini is browned and caramelized, 30-40 minutes. You don't need to turn anything
  3. Once the pan is out of the oven, squeeze a lemon wedge over everything. Garnish with basil and serve with more lemon wedges and red-pepper flakes on the side.
